What is Sheep?

Sheep is a collection of drawings by various folks, prompted with the (random) request: “draw me a sheep.” The request was first motivated by an interest in receiving some recognition of the reference to Antoine de Saint-Exupéry’s The Little Prince, then evolved into a project of collecting idiosyncratic drawings and artifacts of people’s time.

Current collection cycles through 173 drawings from July 2014 to May 2018.
