What is Sheep?

Sheep is a collection of drawings by various folks, prompted with the (random) request: “draw me a sheep.” Inspired by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry’s The Little Prince, the collection began from a curiosity of whether anyone would understand the reference to this work and evolved into an interest in idiosyncratic drawings of sheep and a delight in collecting artifacts of people’s time.

Current collection cycles through 100 drawings from July 2014 to May 2020.
